Hello there! I am the publisher.

Below is my following API:

URL Method Description
/Subscribe/{ip}/{port} GET Subscribe a broker to the raw data.
/Unsubscribe/{ip}/{port} GET Unsubscribe a broker from the raw data.
/Publish GET Generate and send a publication.
/PublishStart/{intervalMs} GET Generate and send publications continuously at the specified interval.
/PublishStop GET Stop the previously started generation and sending of publications.
